Farm Tomita

This chronological table introduces the history of Farm Tomita through photos, from 1897 (Meiji 30), when Tokuma Tomita started plowing the wilderness of Nakafurano. He is the grandfather of Tadao Tomita, who launched lavender cultivation. The production of lavender for fragrances has declined from its peak, due to the growing importation of cheaper fragrances. After harsh days and years, photographers and travelers began to visit the farm, and the Tomitas launched what would become the lavender agronomy of today.
